We use latest high-tech materials

Polymeric electrical insulators

Our product range includes polymeric electrical insulators, selecting the best raw materials on the market. We take care of both the process of theinsulator and the distribution solution, as a Europeaninsulatorcompany, to get full control of the process.

In order to offer all the possible options to our customers, our polymeric insulators have features that facilitate assembly and maintenance, thus saving time and costs for companies. The characteristics of our electrical insulators are:

Hydrophobicity
Self-cleaning
Lightness
Anti-Vandal
Anti-Pollution

Hybrids

Innovative development of our insulators that will only bring advantages and benefits to companies: Hybrid insulators .

They are the result of ceramic insulators on the inside and polymericinsulators on the outside layer, offering hybrid insulation that makes the difference for its technical benefits.

Quality

Quality guarantee

Our products are manufactured for both high and low voltage, and according to UNE, CEI, DIN, UTE , etc. standards, and we currently have the satisfaction of having among our clients the main firms in the national and international market.

Our objective is continuous improvement at all levels and for this we define our quality policy, based on the fact that all manufactured products satisfy both our own expectations and those of our customers, in order to achieve a higher degree of training and participation of all our staff. We currently have the company registration certification according to ISO 9001: 2008 obtained in 2010 through the certification body TÜV NORD- Iberia SL

Controls and tests
